About Me

I am a final-year Master of Computer Science student at the University of Iowa.

My coursework and interests center around data technologies and human-computer interaction, such as statistical learning methods, data privacy, and data visualization. I previously interned for data science and software engineering positions in the industry, and I currently work as TA for CS:2820 Introduction to Software Development and as a Data Analyst for State Farm.

Prior to my Master's, I completed my Computer Science B.S. and Statistics B.A. at the University of Iowa in 2024.

Outside of work and school, I enjoy drawing, indoor rock climbing, playing the piano, and writing.
